## Permissions Explanation

- Record audio: Captures allowed Android playback audio after user approval.
- Media projection foreground service: Keeps the approved capture session active while streaming.
- Internet / network: Discovers and streams to AirPlay-compatible receivers on the local network.
- Wake lock: Keeps playback alive while connected.
- Notifications: Shows an active streaming notification with Stop.
- Modify audio settings: Helps mute local phone playback and sync volume.
